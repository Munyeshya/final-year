## 5) Affordable Housing Availability Finder

### Project Description
A web platform that helps citizens find verified affordable rental housing by area and price range, using transparent and community-informed listings.

### The Problem
Finding affordable housing is difficult and stressful. Prices are unclear, listings are scattered, and many people are overcharged due to lack of information.

### Why It Needs to Be Solved
Access to affordable housing is essential for stable living conditions, financial security, and dignity. Transparency helps protect citizens from exploitation.

### How the Solution Solves It
- Displays affordable rental options by location and price  
- Shows typical rent ranges per area  
- Reduces misinformation and price manipulation  
- Helps people plan housing decisions with confidence  

### Problem Statement
Citizens struggle to find affordable housing due to scattered information and lack of transparency in rental pricing.

### Solution Summary
A housing availability and price-awareness platform that improves transparency and helps citizens locate fair rental options more easily.
