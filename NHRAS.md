## 3) National Health Record Access System
### Project Description
A secure national platform allowing authorized health institutions to access essential patient medical history.
### The Problem
Medical information is scattered across different hospitals and clinics, leading to repeated tests and incomplete care.
### Why It Needs to Be Solved
Accurate patient history improves diagnosis, reduces errors, and enhances treatment quality.
### How the Solution Solves It
- Hospitals securely access verified health records.
- Doctors can see allergies, medication history, and chronic conditions.
- Patient care becomes more consistent across institutions.
### Problem Statement
Healthcare providers lack complete patient information, resulting in ineffective and delayed treatment.
### Solution Summary
A centralized health record system that ensures doctors always have essential patient information for better care.
