## 2) Umudugudu Issue Reporting System
### Project Description
A community platform where residents report local issues—like broken lights, road problems, or water issues—directly to local leaders.
### The Problem
Communication between residents and local leaders is slow or inconsistent, causing unresolved community issues.
### Why It Needs to Be Solved
Faster reporting leads to quicker action, stronger trust, and improved community well-being.
### How the Solution Solves It
- Residents submit issues with descriptions or photos.
- Leaders view, track, and update issue status.
- Community members see progress transparently.
### Problem Statement
Community issues often remain unresolved because residents lack a clear reporting channel.
### Solution Summary
A platform that connects residents and leaders to ensure timely community issue resolution.
