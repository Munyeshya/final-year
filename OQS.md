## 1) Online Queue System (Government Offices)
### Project Description
A web platform that allows citizens to take queue numbers online, view estimated waiting times, and receive notifications when their turn is approaching.
### The Problem
Government offices often have long physical queues that waste citizens' time and cause overcrowding.
### Why It Needs to Be Solved
Reducing physical waiting improves productivity, citizen satisfaction, and service delivery efficiency.
### How the Solution Solves It
- Citizens take queue numbers online.
- They track their turn in real time.
- They receive alerts when service time is near.
### Problem Statement
Citizens lose significant time waiting in unpredictable queues because no digital queue system exists.
### Solution Summary
A digital queueing platform that minimizes waiting time and improves public service experience.
