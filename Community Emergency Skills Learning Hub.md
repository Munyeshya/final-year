## 7) Community Emergency Skills Learning Hub

### Project Description
A web-based learning hub that teaches ordinary citizens simple, life-saving emergency response skills through short, easy-to-understand lessons.

### The Problem
In emergencies, bystanders often panic or do nothing because they lack basic knowledge of what to do before professional help arrives.

### Why It Needs to Be Solved
Early response in emergencies can save lives. Empowering citizens with basic skills improves public safety and preparedness.

### How the Solution Solves It
- Provides short lessons on first aid, accidents, and fire safety  
- Uses simple language and real-life scenarios  
- Focuses on what to do and what not to do  
- Builds confidence, not certification pressure  

### Problem Statement
Many emergencies become more dangerous because citizens lack basic emergency response knowledge during the critical first minutes.

### Solution Summary
A practical learning platform that equips citizens with essential emergency response knowledge, helping save lives before professional help arrives.
